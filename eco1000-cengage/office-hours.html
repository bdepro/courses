How to Sign Up

Send an email ahead of time

If you'd like to meet during office hours, send me an email before we meet. In a perfect world, 24 hours' notice helps us both plan and gives me time to open the Zoom room for you. I'll reply to confirm the time and send a quick reminder before we meet.

If none of the scheduled times work for you, just say so in your email and we'll find something that fits.

Subject: Office Hours Request – Wednesday
Hi Prof. Depro,

I'd like to meet during office hours on Wednesday if you're free. I have a few questions about the week's assignment and some planning for next semester.

Thanks,
[Your Name]

What Can We Talk About?

Anything that helps you move forward

There's no wrong reason to come to office hours. Some students arrive with specific questions about the week's assignment. Others want to think through bigger decisions. A few just want to check in.

  • Questions about chapter readings, videos, or MindTap assignments
  • Help understanding a concept before attempting a badge or puzzle
  • Course registration and planning for future semesters
  • Exploring options in economics, consulting, or related fields
  • Anything else on your mind — just send a note and we'll go from there
Can't make it to office hours? Send me a message through Moodle or email and we'll find another time. I want to make sure you have the support you need — the schedule is a starting point, not a barrier.
