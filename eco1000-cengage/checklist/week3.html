This Week's Big Idea

Markets are powerful — but they don't produce equal outcomes for everyone. This week you'll examine why political incentives often lead to policies that fail, why labor markets reward some workers more than others, and what economics actually tells us about inequality and poverty. These are the questions that will follow you into every career and every civic conversation you'll ever have.
