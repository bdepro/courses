How Economies Perform and Grow

This Week's Big Idea

Last week you learned how the market process determines prices. This week you zoom out to ask a bigger question: how do we measure whether an entire economy is healthy, and why do some nations grow wealthy while others fall behind? The answers shape every policy debate you'll encounter for the rest of your life.
