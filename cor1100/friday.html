Part 1 Written Narrative

Friday Focus asks one question: what do Hanley and Smith say about something you actually care about? You spend the semester building your answer.

Write it as an argument, for a reader who has never taken this course. You have the whole semester to work on it, and three Fridays are set aside for that purpose. Come to those classes with something on the page.
