Classroom Policy

AI Policy for This Course
No assignment may be generated entirely by AI.

AI can be part of how you work — brainstorming, drafting, checking your logic — but the final submission needs to be yours.

Revise carefully. If you use AI, the ideas and voice in what you hand in should be recognizably your own. You will write short reflections on your AI use, and we will practice this together in class.

If you are unsure whether something is appropriate, ask before you submit. That conversation is easy. The other one is not.

This policy applies to all written work, check-ins, and assignments in this course unless otherwise specified on the assignment itself.
