Help & Support

Campus resources available to every Elon student

Campus Resources

Learning Assistance
The Koenigsberger Learning Center offers tutoring, success coaching, and academic support. Email learningassistance@elon.edu to get started.
Academic Accommodations
Contact the Office of Academic Accommodations and Accessibility at 336.278.6568 or accommodations@elon.edu to arrange reasonable accommodations.
Belk Library
Librarians help you find sources and research tools. Book an appointment or chat directly on the library website.
Writing Center
On the first floor of Belk Library. The Writing Center offers individual appointments, multimedia studios, and presentation practice rooms.
Religious Holidays
Students observing religious holidays must submit the Religious Observance Notification Form by September 8, 2026.
Honor Code
Elon's honor pledge calls for Honesty, Integrity, Responsibility, and Respect. Read the code of conduct policies in the student handbook.
Additional Resources
Student Care & Outreach handles concerns about classmates. Enrollment and withdrawal policies are managed through OnTrack. E-Warnings communicate academic performance early. Consult the Academic Catalog, Student Handbook, and Academic Calendar for complete university policies.
