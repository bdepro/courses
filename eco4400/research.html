Applied Research & Case AnalyticsData Intensive 35%
Three independent assignments develop your analytical skills alongside the consulting project. Each assignment has its own standard of evidence: the replication study is judged on whether your analysis is right, the case assignments on whether your position is defensible. Each is examined orally after you submit it, either as a presentation to the class or a one-on-one meeting with your instructors.
1
Journal Replication Project
Take a published study on housing and energy use, re-run the analysis on the original data, and write up what you find. Say plainly where your results match the published estimate and where they don’t.
SLO 3 — Methods & limitations SLO 5 — Software analysis
2
Case Analysis 1
A business case that puts a real decision in front of you. Your memo takes a defensible position, applies the relevant economic frameworks from the course, and cites evidence from the case itself.
SLO 3 — Economic reasoning
3
Case Analysis 2 — Strategic Games
Strategic decision-making applied to an industry where payoffs are measurable and the strategies are explicit. Identify the game structure, the dominant strategies (if any), and what the equilibrium outcome predicts. Connect the analysis to the game theory concepts from Weeks 8–9.
Ch. 15 — Strategic Games
AI reflection requirement. Each case submission includes a short reflection on how you used AI tools in the analysis—what you asked, what it produced, and what you changed. Details on the reflection format will be provided in class.
