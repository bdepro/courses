Friday Focus

25% of your course grade · written narrative + viva conversation · sign up before Thanksgiving

Part 1 Written Narrative

Friday Focus asks one question: what does economics explain about something you actually care about? You spend the semester building your answer.

Write it as an argument about a real economic phenomenon, for a reader who has never taken economics. You have the whole semester to work on it, and three Friday class sessions set aside for that purpose. Come to those sessions with something on the page.

Submit as a Canvas assignment. Due Wednesday, December 2 at 11:59 p.m.

Part 2 Viva Conversation

The viva is a short conversation with me about what you wrote. Think of it as a chance to talk through your argument and explain the choices you made. It is not an oral exam.

You sign up for a slot during viva week, November 30 through December 4. If you need to defer, exam period slots are available December 7 through 11.

Sign up before Thanksgiving break. Slots fill. Use a computer — the Canvas scheduler does not work reliably on phones. No computer access? Contact me and I will assign your slot.
